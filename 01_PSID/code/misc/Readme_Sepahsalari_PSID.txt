Structure of the Repository:

Data Wrangling Scripts (Julia): 
Within the 'do file' directory,you will find Julia scripts meticulously designed for data preparation and cleaning. 

PSID Data (Data Files): 
The 'data file' directory houses the Panel Study of Income Dynamics (PSID) data. 

Graphical Visualizations (Graphs): 
In the 'graph' directory, I have compiled a series of visual representations of our findings.
