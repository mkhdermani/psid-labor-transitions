	\section{Introduction}
	
	In this study, I use data from the Current Population Survey (CPS) to compare with the National Longitudinal Survey of Youth 1979 (NLSY79) and the National Longitudinal Survey of Youth 1997 (NLSY97). I also apply your formula to compare the data as part of the analysis.
	
	Furthermore, I compare the results obtained from the NLSY79 and NLSY97 datasets with the findings of Fallick and Fleischman. I follow their methodology by using the mean average for the comparison, as they utilized this approach in their study.
	
	To facilitate easy comparison, I have compiled the mean transition rates in a table, which summarizes the key findings.
